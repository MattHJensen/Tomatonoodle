## How to Learn from Videos

### Active Watching Method

```
First Watch:
- Watch entire video without pausing
- Get overall flow and rhythm
- Note questions

Second Watch:
- Pause at each technique
- Take notes on timing
- Note temperatures and visual cues

Third Watch:
- Cook along with video
- Pause as needed
- Compare your results
```

### Key Visual Cues to Watch For

| Stage | What to See |
|-------|-------------|
| Oil ready | Shimmers, slight smoke |
| Aromatics done | Fragrant, light golden |
| Tomatoes ready | Oil red, paste-like |
| Soup simmering | Gentle bubbles, not rolling |
| Eggs done | Tender ribbons, not tough |
| Noodles ready | Al dente, slight bite |
