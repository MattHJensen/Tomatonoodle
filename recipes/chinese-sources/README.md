## 美食杰 (Meishij)

### "玉池桃红的西红柿鸡蛋面"
**English:** "Yuchitaohong's Tomato Egg Noodles"

**Source:** [美食杰](https://www.meishij.net/zuofa/xihongshijidanmian.html)

#### 描述 | Description

> "西红柿鸡蛋龙须面，是最家常的，也是最普通的一种汤面了。虽然是家常的，但非常的美味可口哦，特别是西红柿经过油炒，红红亮亮的汤，酸中带香，那种味道很独特。"

**English Translation:** "Tomato egg dragon beard noodles is the most homestyle and ordinary soup noodle. Although it's homestyle, it's very delicious. Especially when tomatoes are stir-fried in oil, the soup becomes bright red, sour with fragrance, a unique taste."

#### 食材 | Ingredients

| 中文 | English | 用量 |
|------|---------|------|
| 番茄 | Tomato | 1个 |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 |
| 面条 | Noodles | 150g |
| 葱姜蒜 | Scallion, ginger, garlic | 适量 |
| 花生油 | Peanut oil | 适量 |

#### 特殊技巧 | Special Technique

1. **炒鸡蛋时不要炒老，大火迅速翻炒，凝固后立刻盛出**
   - Don't overcook eggs - use high heat, stir quickly, remove immediately when set

2. **番茄炒蛋除了盐不需要加任何调料（酱油、鸡精都不要）**
   - Besides salt, don't add any other seasonings (no soy sauce, no MSG)

## 豆果美食 (Douguo)

### Recipe A: "番茄鸡蛋面" (汤面版)

**Source:** [豆果美食](https://www.douguo.com/cookbook/2062175.html)

#### 食材 | Ingredients

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 西红柿 | Tomatoes (3-4) |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs (1 per person) |
| 家常面条 | Home-style noodles |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallions |
| 青菜 | Green vegetables |

#### 做法 | Cooking Steps

1. **西红柿去皮切块** - Peel and dice tomatoes
2. **热锅冷油放葱花和番茄块** - Hot pan, cold oil, add scallions and tomato pieces
3. **加少许盐翻炒番茄成浓稠番茄汁** - Add a little salt, stir-fry until thick tomato sauce
4. **加水煮开后下鸡蛋、面条、青菜** - Add water, bring to boil, add eggs, noodles, vegetables

## 关键技巧 | Essential Techniques

### 1. 番茄去皮方法 | Tomato Peeling Methods

| 方法 | Method | 中文 | Description |
|------|--------|------|-------------|
| 开水烫法 | Boiling water | 在底部划十字，开水烫后轻松去皮 | Score X on bottom, blanch in boiling water, peel easily |
| 筷子火烧法 | Chopstick + flame | 筷子插入蒂部，在火上转动直到皮裂开 | Insert chopstick in stem, rotate over flame until skin cracks |
| 勺子刮皮法 | Spoon scraping | 用勺子刮遍整个表面，然后去皮 | Scrape entire surface with spoon, then peel |
| 冷冻法 | Freezing | -15至-18°C冷冻3小时，解冻后去皮 | Freeze for 3 hours at -15 to -18°C, thaw and peel |

### 2. "炒出沙"技巧 | "Stir-fry Until Sandy" Technique

这是获得浓郁番茄风味的关键:
This is the key to rich tomato flavor:

| 技巧 | Technique | 说明 | Explanation |
|------|-----------|------|-------------|
| 加盐快速出沙 | Add salt | 帮助番茄快速出汁 | Help tomatoes release juice quickly |
| 小火慢炒 | Low heat | 慢慢炒至糊状 | Slowly cook until paste-like |
| 用铲子压 | Press with spatula | 帮助分解番茄 | Help break down tomatoes |
| 油变红色 | Red oil | 油变红色说明炒好了 | Oil should turn red when properly done |

### 3. 鸡蛋技巧 | Egg Techniques

## 挑选西红柿 | Choosing Tomatoes

### 好番茄 | Good Tomatoes

- **形状扁圆** - Flat, round shape
- **颜色红润** - Deep red color
- **轻按有点软** - Slightly soft when pressed
- **掰开果肉鲜红，籽呈土黄色** - Red flesh, yellowish seeds
- **汁水多，口感"沙"** - Juicy, sandy texture

### 避免 | Avoid

- **底高或有棱角** - High bottom or angular shape
- **激素催熟的西红柿** - Artificially ripened tomatoes
- **无籽或籽呈绿色** - No seeds or green seeds
- **发硬发涩** - Hard and astringent

## 地方特色对比 | Regional Variations

### 北方特色 | Northern Style

- **汤底:** 使用煮面原汤 (原汤)
- **面条:** 偏爱手擀面或刀切面
- **调味:** 可能加番茄酱增味（北方番茄汁水较少）
- **口感:** 过冷水让面条更筋道
- **风格:** 打卤面 - 浓稠肉卤浇在面上

### 南方特色 | Southern Style

- **汤底:** 先在碗中准备调味料和热水，再加面条
- **面条:** 偏爱龙须面或细挂面
- **风格:** 捞面 - 沥干面条配酱汁，汤少
- **口感:** 面条煮得更软

### 成都特色: 华兴街番茄煎蛋面

**独特之处:**
- 使用煎蛋而非炒蛋
- 非常熟的番茄炒成糊状做浓汤
- 煎蛋时摊开蛋黄增加蛋的体积
