# Visual Archive

## Purpose

Visual documentation is essential for food history. This document:
1. Catalogs known historical images
2. Describes what we're seeking
3. Provides standards for image documentation
4. Lists resources for visual research

## V. Image Documentation Standards

### Required Metadata

Every image in the archive must include:

```
Filename: [Descriptive, with date]
Title: [Clear description of image content]
Date: [When taken/created]
Creator: [Photographer/artist/source]
Location: [Where taken, if known]
Description: [Detailed description of what's shown]
Copyright: [Status and permissions]
Source: [Where the image was obtained]
Archive ID: [Unique identifier]
```

### Example Entry

```
Filename: 1985-chengdu-huaxing-noodle-stall.jpg
Title: Late-night noodle stall on Huaxing Street, Chengdu
Date: Approximately 1985
Creator: Unknown
Location: Huaxing Street, Chengdu, Sichuan Province
Description: Black and white photograph showing a street-side
  noodle stall with plastic stools and steaming pots. Two
  customers eating from bowls. Vendor visible behind counter.
  Sign partially visible. Typical late-night noodle scene.
Copyright: Unknown; seeking rights information
Source: [Where obtained]
Archive ID: PHOTO-1985-001
```

### Image Quality Standards

| Type | Minimum Resolution | Format |
|------|-------------------|--------|
| Archival scan | 300 DPI | TIFF or PNG |
| Web display | 1200px wide | JPEG or PNG |
| Thumbnail | 400px wide | JPEG |

## VIII. Copyright and Permissions

### Copyright Status by Era

| Period | Likely Status | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Pre-1949 | Complex | May be public domain or unclear |
| 1949-1990 | State ownership likely | Chinese government copyright |
| Post-1990 | Individual copyright | Normal copyright applies |

### Obtaining Permissions

1. **Identify copyright holder** - Creator, estate, or institution
2. **Contact for permission** - Written request preferred
3. **Document permission** - Keep records of agreements
4. **Note restrictions** - Any limitations on use

### Fair Use Considerations

Educational and archival use may allow:
- Commentary and criticism
- Educational purposes
- Research and scholarship
- Non-commercial use

**Note:** Fair use varies by jurisdiction; when in doubt, obtain permission.

## IX. Contributing Images

### What We Accept

| Type | Requirements |
|------|--------------|
| Personal photographs | You own or have rights |
| Family archives | With family permission |
| Institutional images | With proper permissions |
| Public domain | Verified public domain status |

### How to Submit

1. **Scan or photograph** at highest reasonable quality
2. **Complete metadata** per standards above
3. **Document permissions** - Your rights or obtained permissions
4. **Submit via pull request** or contact maintainers

### What We Cannot Accept

- Images without clear copyright status
- Images taken without consent
- Commercially copyrighted images without permission
- Digitally altered images (without disclosure)
